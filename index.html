    What I've Completed

    Neural Network Library

    While working for Ericsson in Ottawa, I collaborated within a team of 10 to develop and ship an in-house neural network library in C, optimized for Ericsson Baseband Architecture. Our goal was to mimic and preserve the ease-of-use and functionality of tensorflow while optimizing inference runtime & weight/bias storage usage. Most of my activities within this project involved quantizing weights and biases, and storing as many of these values as possible within a single memory address. Naturally, there is an accuracy tradeoff the smaller you quantize.

    Student Housing Hub

    I created a Student Housing Portal to assist Queen's University students find off-campus housing. At the time of creation, there was only a facebook marketplace groupchat being used to matchmake landlords and students. This platform enabled landlords to post rental units, and students to filter them by bedrooms to find the place that fit their group the best. Developed using NextJS, React, and Firebase.
